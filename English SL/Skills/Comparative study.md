## Think about...
- **visual art in context** - examine and compare works of artists. Consider the context influencing their own works and the works of others
- **methods** - Look at different techniques for making art. Explore how and why different techniques have evolved and the processes involved
- **communicating arts** - explore ways of communicating through visual and written means. How artistic choices are made to most effectively communicate the idea or knowledge
- Look at various primary and secondary source
