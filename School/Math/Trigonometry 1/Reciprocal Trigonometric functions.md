
- Cosecant: $csc(x) = \dfrac 1{sin(x)}$
- Secant: $sec(x) = \dfrac 1{cos(x)}$
- Cotangent: $cot(x) = \dfrac 1{tan(x)}$

1. $sec^2(x) = tan^2 (x) + 1$
2. $csc^2 (x) = cot^2(x) + 1$
